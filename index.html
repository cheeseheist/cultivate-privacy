Privacy Policy

Effective date: [FILL IN DATE]

Cultivate ("the App," "we," "us") is built around a simple idea: help you be intentional about the relationships that matter to you. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it's used, and the choices you have.

The short version: everything you enter into Cultivate — the people you track, your notes, goals, and photos — stays on your device. We do not operate a server that stores your personal content, and we do not sell or share your data. The only information that leaves your device is a small set of anonymous, aggregate usage signals described below, which are never linked to your identity.

1. Information You Provide

When you use Cultivate, you may enter information such as relationship names, photos, notes, goals, special dates, and activity logs. This information is stored entirely on your device using Apple's on-device SwiftData storage framework. We do not have a backend server, and this information is never transmitted to us or to any third party.

Because this data lives on your device, it is included in your device's standard iCloud backup if you have that enabled — the same way your other apps' data is backed up. We do not have access to your iCloud backups.

2. Automatically Collected Information (Analytics)

Cultivate uses a privacy-focused analytics service called TelemetryDeck to understand which parts of the App people find useful, so we know where to invest our development time. This is limited to anonymous, aggregate signals such as:

  • The type of relationship created (e.g., "Friend," "Wife," "Mentor") and its priority tier
  • The category or trait of a plan applied (e.g., "Marriage," "Courage — Ages 9–11") — never the plan's private title or your notes
  • The category of a brainstorm goal saved
  • Whether a goal was completed, and its recurrence type

These signals never include:

  • Names, photos, or any personal content you've entered
  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) or any persistent identifier tied to you
  • Your location, contacts, or any data outside the App itself

Each signal is independent — we do not set any user identifier that would let us connect two signals together as "the same person," and TelemetryDeck hashes technical identifiers on your device before anything is sent, so nothing is linked back to your identity. Because of this, Cultivate does not need to show Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt — this analytics approach does not meet Apple's definition of "tracking."

3. Purchases

Cultivate offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium features. All payment is processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never see, collect, or store your payment card details, billing address, or Apple ID — Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

4. Notifications

Cultivate can send you local reminders (for example, about an upcoming goal or special day). These are scheduled entirely on your device using Apple's notification framework. No notification content or scheduling data is sent to us or to any server.

5. Photos

If you choose to add a photo to a relationship, it is selected using Apple's standard photo picker, stored locally in the App's on-device database, and never uploaded anywhere. We never request or receive access to your full photo library.

7. Children's Privacy

Cultivate is intended for use by adults who want to be intentional about their relationships — including relationships with their own children. The App is not directed at children under 13, does not require creation of an account, and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Any information about a child (for example, tracking a parent's relationship with their son or daughter) is entered voluntarily by the adult user and stored only on that adult's own device.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Your content: since all relationship data stays on your device, you have complete control over it. Deleting the App deletes this data. You can also delete individual relationships or entries at any time within the App.
  • Analytics: because the usage signals described in Section 2 are anonymous and not linked to your identity from the moment they're created, we have no way to identify which signals came from you specifically, and therefore cannot access, export, or delete an individual's analytics history on request — there is nothing identifying to look up.
  • EU/UK users: if you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you may have additional rights under the GDPR/UK GDPR. TelemetryDeck's infrastructure is operated to be compliant with these regulations. Because we do not collect information that identifies you, most individual-rights requests (access, correction, erasure) do not apply to data we hold, as we do not hold data that identifies you.

9. Data Retention

Your relationship content is retained on your device for as long as you keep the App installed, or until you delete it within the App. Anonymous usage signals are retained by TelemetryDeck according to their standard retention practices; since they are not linked to your identity, they cannot be individually deleted on request.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time — for example, if we add a new feature that changes what information is collected. We'll update the effective date above when we do. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
