Privacy Policy

App: Tend – Relationship Tracker
Developer: AllReady Apps
Effective date: May 2, 2026

Overview

Tend is designed with your privacy in mind. All data you enter stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

All relationship data, goals, activity logs, and photos you add are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is included in your device's standard iCloud backup if you have iCloud backup enabled — this is managed entirely by Apple and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

Tend offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium features. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Local Notifications

Tend may send local notifications to remind you to connect with people. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not routed through any external server.

Children's Privacy

Tend is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
